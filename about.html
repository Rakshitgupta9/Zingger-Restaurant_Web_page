About us

At Zinnger, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey that celebrates the rich and diverse flavors of Jammu. Our restaurant is a haven for food enthusiasts seeking a taste of authentic local cuisine fused with modern culinary techniques. Nestled in the heart of Jammu, Zinnger offers a warm and inviting ambiance, perfect for family gatherings, romantic dinners, or casual outings with friends. Our menu is a symphony of tantalizing flavors, showcasing the finest ingredients sourced from the region. From delectable Rogan Josh to the fragrant Dum Aloo, our chefs have mastered the art of preparing traditional dishes that will transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Jammu. At Zinnger, we pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, service, and the dining experience. Whether you're a local resident or a traveler exploring the flavors of Jammu, our team is dedicated to providing you with a memorable and satisfying meal every time you visit. Join us at Zinnger, and let us take you on a culinary adventure that captures the essence of Jammu's culinary heritage. We look forward to serving you and making your dining experience truly unforgettable. Reserve your table today and savor the magic of Zinnger!
